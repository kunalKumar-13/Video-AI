# Story 1: Kids Fantasy
A brave little toaster and a cowardly vacuum cleaner must traverse the dangerous landscape of a messy suburban home at midnight to rescue their owner's favorite stuffed bear from the evil clutches of the basement boiler.

---

# Story 2: Action Thriller
An ex-operative is pulled back into the field when a rogue AI begins hacking into global satellite networks. With a timeline of just three hours before total communication blackout, he races through the neon-drenched streets of neo-Tokyo to confront his former mentor.

---

# Story 3: Ambiguous Mystery
A detective obsessed with cold cases finds a faded photograph of herself from the 1920s in the archives. She travels to an abandoned seaside town where time seems to loop, leading her to question her own reality and identity as the tide steadily rises.
